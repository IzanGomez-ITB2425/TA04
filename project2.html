Disks Presentation

For this project, my team and I developed and designed an oral presentation about disks and their importance in the world of computing. We focused on different types of disks, their functionalities, and how to choose the right disk for specific needs.

The presentation covered topics such as HDDs, SSDs, and the latest advancements in storage technology. Our goal was to educate users on making informed decisions when selecting storage devices for their computers.
